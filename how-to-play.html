How to play Guess Poo!

The game is pretty straight forward

Once you click the "Start" button, You will then begin! You will be given ten questions — one after the other. All of which will be asking you to identify one thing...

Who does the poo belong to?

Each question will have a different picture of a poo belonging to an aninimal in particular, and beneath will be 4 options to choose from (4 different animal names).

Only one option will be correct.

Once an answer is selected, a next button will appear underneath the answers to take you to the next question.

Your final score will be provided once you have completed all ten and having pressed the 'See Results' button.

Let's see if you really know your. . . stuff!
